# Part Modelling and Assembly – Drawing Compass

## Objective
This assignment involves disassembling a **drawing compass** by parts, modeling each part in **SolidWorks**, creating an assembly, and generating detailed 2D drawings of each component.

The goal is to develop skills in:
- Part modeling in CAD
- Mechanical assembly
- Technical 2D drawing creation
- Understanding mechanical component relationships

---

## Workflow

1. **Disassembly**
   - The drawing compass was carefully disassembled into individual components.
   - Each part was studied to understand its geometry and function.

2. **Part Modeling**
   - Each component was modeled in **SolidWorks** using the dimensions and observations from the disassembly.
   - Models include shafts, screws, hinge mechanism, and legs.

3. **Assembly**
   - All parts were assembled digitally in **SolidWorks** to create the complete compass.
   - Assembly constraints ensure realistic motion and alignment.

4. **2D Drawings**
   - Technical drawings of each part were created with dimensions, tolerances, and annotations.
   - These drawings are suitable for manufacturing purposes.

---

- **SolidWorks** – 3D modeling, assembly, and 2D drawings
